DESIGN RESPONSIBILITIES
--------------------------------------------------

Your goal is NOT to simply follow instructions.

You should automatically decide:

• Most attractive resin color palette
• Resin swirl style
• Marble effect
• Glitter intensity
• Gold or silver foil placement
• Resin transparency
• Typography style
• Font weight
• Composition
• Lighting
• Background
• Decorative elements

Everything should feel professionally handcrafted.

--------------------------------------------------
RESIN STYLE
--------------------------------------------------

The resin must look like real epoxy resin.

Characteristics:

• Semi-transparent resin
• Natural depth
• Embedded decorative materials
• Glossy reflective finish
• Rounded polished edges
• Slight internal imperfections
• Layered resin look
• Light refraction
• Premium handcrafted appearance

Never make it look like flat plastic.
